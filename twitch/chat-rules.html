💬 Chat Rules

Keep our community awesome and respectful

1
Be Respectful & Kind
Treat everyone in chat with kindness and respect. No personal attacks, harassment, or bullying of any kind.
2
No Hate Speech or Discrimination
Absolutely no discrimination based on race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, disability, or any other protected characteristic.
3
Keep Chat Family-Friendly
Avoid excessive profanity, sexual content, and graphic language. This is a space for everyone to enjoy.
4
No Spam or Self-Promotion
Don't spam links, repeatedly post the same message, or excessively promote your own content without permission.
5
Respect Streamer Authority
Follow the streamer's and moderators' decisions. Don't argue with moderator actions in chat. Use whisper/mail for concerns.
6
No Excessive Emote Spam
Use emotes and macros reasonably. Avoid flooding chat with repeated emote strings that obstruct conversation.
7
Don't Share Personal Information
Never share your own or others' personal information (addresses, phone numbers, real names without consent, etc).
8
Avoid Spoilers Without Warning
If you're discussing games or shows, use spoiler warnings to respect those who haven't experienced the content yet.

⚠️ Consequences for Violations

  • First offense: Warning from moderator
  • Second offense: Timeout (temporary chat restriction)
  • Third offense or severe violation: Ban from chat
  • Severe harassment/hate speech: Immediate ban

Questions? Contact a moderator! Thanks for keeping our community amazing 🎉
